In The Heart of Miraj

Mokkaa Coffee is Miraj's premium café experience — nestled within the Sanjay Bhokare Group of Institutes campus in Parshwanath Nagar. We combine exceptional coffee with comfortable spaces designed for connection, creativity, and calm.

Every detail — from our carefully curated drinks to our warm, welcoming interiors — reflects our commitment to more than just a beverage. It's about creating a space where guests can relax, connect, and enjoy meaningful moments.

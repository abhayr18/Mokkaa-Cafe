THE MOKKAA STORY

Miraj's premium café — guided by quality, crafted with warmth, and rooted in community.

Our Beginning

Born From A Love
Of Exceptional Coffee

Mokkaa Coffee was born with one simple belief: that a great cup of coffee, served in a beautiful space, can change your entire day. Nestled inside the Sanjay Bhokare Group of Institutes Faculty of Engineering campus in Parshwanath Nagar, Miraj — we set out to create something special for this community.

We've grown into a destination — a place where students, faculty, and locals gather, ideas are exchanged, and every guest leaves feeling genuinely nourished. The warm, modern interiors and the aroma of freshly brewed coffee create an atmosphere unlike anywhere else in Miraj.

We obsess over every detail, from the quality of our beans to the consistency of every pour. We believe that hospitality is an art form, and we practice it with every order.
